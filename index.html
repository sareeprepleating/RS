Inverted pleating

Fold inwards towards the center of a garment, creating a smooth look.

Knife Pleats

Folds of fabric that are pressed in one direction on the right of the fabric

Box Pleats

Made up of two knife pleats facing away from each other.They are often used on skirst or dresses
